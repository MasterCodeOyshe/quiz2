**1. "把" (bǎ) Sentences for Handling Objects**

- **Structure description:** To make this sentence negative, you simply place **"没" (méi)** directly before the word "把" to show that the action was not performed on the object.
- **Example:** _Wǒ méi bǎ shǒujī fàng zài zhuōzi shang._ (I didn't put the cellphone on the table.).

**2. "会" (huì) Sentences for Future Possibility**

- **Structure description:** Place **"不" (bù)** right before "会" to express that an action or event will _not_ happen.
- **Example:** _Wǒ xià ge xīngqī bú huì qù Shànghǎi._ (I will not go to Shanghai next week.).

**3. "了" (le) Sentences for Completed Actions**

- **Structure description:** Place **"没" (méi)** before the verb. When you do this, you must **drop the "了" (le)** from the sentence, and you also usually remove the number and measure word that described the object.
- **Example:** _Wǒ méi kàn Zhōngwén diànyǐng._ (I didn't see a Chinese movie.).

**4. "比" (bǐ) Sentences for Comparisons**

- **Structure description:** Replace the word "比" (bǐ) with **"没有" (méiyǒu)**. This changes the sentence from saying "A is more than B" to "A is _not as_ [Adjective] as B".
- **Example:** _Wǒ méiyǒu tā gāo._ (I'm not as tall as him.).

**5. Question Words + "也/都" (yě/dōu) for "None"**

- **Structure description:** This structure uses a question word (like "what" or "where") followed by "也" (yě) or "都" (dōu), and then adds the negative word **"没" (méi) or "不" (bù)** before the verb. This translates to "nothing," "no one," or "nowhere".
- **Example:** _Yéye shénme yě méi mǎi._ (Grandpa bought nothing.).

**6. Double-Verb (Pivotal) Sentences**

- **Structure description:** To make this negative, place the negative word **"没" (méi)** or **"不" (bù)** directly before the _first_ verb (words like "ask," "invite," or "let").
- **Example:** _Lǎoshī méi jiào wǒ huídá zhège wèntí._ (The teacher didn't ask me to answer this question.)

**7. Sentences Showing Action Frequency**

- **Structure description:** Place **"没" (méi)** before the verb, and usually drop the "了" (le), to state that you did not complete the action a certain number of times.
- **Example:** _Wǒ méi dǎ sān cì diànhuà._ (I didn't call three times.)

**8. Sentences Showing Action Duration**

- **Structure description:** Place **"没" (méi)** before the verb and drop the "了" (le) to indicate that an action did not last for the specified period of time.
- **Example:** _Líu lǎoshī méi shuì bā ge xiǎoshí._ (Teacher Liu didn't sleep for eight hours.)

**9. "跟" (gēn) Sentences for "With" or "From"**

- **Structure description:** Place the negative word **"没" (méi)** or **"不" (bù)** directly in front of the word "跟" (gēn) to show that an action was _not_ done with someone.
- **Example:** _Wǒ méi gēn lǎoshī xué Hànyǔ._ (I didn't study Chinese with the teacher.)
