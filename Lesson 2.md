|**1**|Question|Nǐ shì shénme shíhou qù Xī'ān de?|When did you go to Xi'an?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì Qīyuè shí'èr hào qù Xī'ān de.|I went to Xi'an on July 12th.|
|**2**|Question|Nǐ shì shénme shíhou lái Zhōngguó de?|When did you come to China?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì jīnnián lái Zhōngguó de.|I came to China this year.|
|**3**|Question|Nǐ shì shénme shíhou lái bàngōngshì de?|When did you come to the office?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì qī diǎn bàn lái bàngōngshì de.|I came to the office at 7:30.|
|**4**|Question|Nǐ shì shénme shíhou qù dàshǐguǎn de?|When did you go to the embassy?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì qiántiān qù dàshǐguǎn de.|I went to the embassy the day before yesterday.|
|**5**|Question|Nǐ shì zěnme qù Yìdàlì de?|How did you go to Italy?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì zuò fēijī qù Yìdàlì de.|I went to Italy by airplane.|
|**6**|Question|Nǐ shì zěnme qù Gùgōng de?|How did you go to the Imperial Palace?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì kāi chē qù Gùgōng de.|I drove to the Imperial Palace.|
|**7**|Question|Nǐ shì zěnme qù Yíhé Yuán de?|How did you go to the Summer Palace?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì zuò gōnggòng qìchē qù Yíhé Yuán de.|I went to the Summer Palace by public bus.|
|**8**|Question|Nǐ shì gēn shéi yìqǐ qù pá shān de?|Who did you go climbing a mountain with?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì gēn péngyou yìqǐ qù pá shān de.|I went climbing a mountain with a friend.|
|**9**|Question|Nǐ shì gēn shéi yìqǐ qù chī fàn de?|Who did you go to eat with?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì gēn kèren yìqǐ qù chī fàn de.|I went to eat with a guest.|
|**10**|Question|Nǐ shì zài nǎr kàn jiàn Ālǐ de?|Where did you see Ali?|
||Answer|Wǒ shì zài kāfēiguǎnr kàn jiàn Ālǐ de.|I saw Ali at the coffee shop.|
