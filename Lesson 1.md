
|Word (Chinese)|Pinyin|English Translation|
|:--|:--|:--|
|**最近**|zuìjìn|recently, lately|
|**西安**|Xī'ān|Xi'an, capital of Shaanxi Province|
|**时候**|shíhou|time, moment|
|**跟**|gēn|with|
|**一起**|yìqǐ|together|
|**回**|huí|to return, to come or go back|
|**方便**|fāngbiàn|convenient|
|**便宜**|piányi|cheap, inexpensive|
|**因为**|yīnwèi|because|

| Speaker            | Pinyin                                                                                            | English Translation                                                             |
| :----------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Ālǐ, nǐ zuìjìn qù nǎr lǚyóu le?                                                                   | Ali, where did you travel recently?                                             |
| **Ali**            | Wǒ qù Xī'ān le.                                                                                   | I went to Xi'an.                                                                |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Nǐ shì shénme shíhou qù Xī'ān de?                                                                 | When did you go to Xi'an?                                                       |
| **Ali**            | Wǒ shì Qīyuè shí'èr hào qù Xī'ān de.                                                              | I went there on July 12th.                                                      |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Nǐ shì zěnme qù Xī'ān de?                                                                         | How did you go to Xi'an?                                                        |
| **Ali**            | Wǒ shì zuò fēijī qù de, zuò fēijī kuài.                                                           | I went there by plane. It's faster.                                             |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Nǐ shì gēn shéi yìqǐ qù de?                                                                       | Who did you go with?                                                            |
| **Ali**            | Wǒ shì gēn Běnjiémíng hé Jīn Měiyīng yìqǐ qù de.                                                  | I went with Benjamin and Miyong Kim.                                            |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Nǐ shì zěnme huí Běijīng de?                                                                      | How did you come back to Beijing?                                               |
| **Ali**            | Wǒ shì zuótiān gēn Běnjiémíng yìqǐ zuò huǒchē huí Běijīng de, zuò huǒchē fāngbiàn, yě hěn piányi. | I came back with Benjamin by train yesterday. It's more convenient and cheaper. |
| **Liu Xiaoshuang** | Nǐmen shì zěnme huí xuéxiào de?                                                                   | Then how did you get to school?                                                 |
| **Ali**            | Wǒmen shì zǒu huí xuéxiào de, yīnwèi méi qián le.                                                 | We walked back to school, because we had no money left.                         |
