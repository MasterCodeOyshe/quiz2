|Dialogue|Pinyin|English Translation|
|:--|:--|:--|
|**1 (Example)**|Q: Tāmen lái de zǎo ma?A: Wáng mìshū lái de hěn zǎo, Zhāng jīnglǐ lái de hěn wǎn.|Q: Do they arrive early?A: Secretary Wang arrives very early, Manager Zhang arrives very late.|
|**2**|Q: Tāmen zǒu de kuài ma?A: Gēge zǒu de hěn kuài, dìdi zǒu de hěn màn.|Q: Do they walk fast?A: Older brother walks very fast, younger brother walks very slowly.|
|**3**|Q: Tāmen chī de duō ma?A: Bàba chī de hěn duō, māma chī de hěn shǎo.|Q: Do they eat a lot?A: Dad eats a lot, mom eats very little.|
|**4**|Q: Tāmen zhù de yuǎn ma?A: Liú lǎoshī zhù de hěn yuǎn, Zhāng lǎoshī zhù de hěn jìn.|Q: Do they live far away?A: Teacher Liu lives very far, Teacher Zhang lives very close.|
|**5 (Example)**|Q: Cài zuò de hǎochī bu hǎochī?A: Zhège cài zuò de hěn hǎochī, nàge cài zuò de bù hǎochī.|Q: Is the dish cooked deliciously?A: This dish is cooked very deliciously, that dish is not cooked deliciously.|
|**6**|Q: Zhàopiàn zhào de hǎokàn bu hǎokàn?A: Zhè zhāng zhàopiàn zhào de hěn hǎokàn, nà zhāng zhàopiàn zhào de bù hǎokàn.|Q: Is the photo taken well?A: This photo is taken very well, that photo is not taken well.|
|**7 (Source Example)**|Q: Tāmen Hànyǔ shuō de zěnmeyàng?A: Ānnī Hànyǔ shuō de hěn liúlì, Dàwèi Hànyǔ shuō de bù liúlì.|Q: How do they speak Chinese?A: Annie speaks Chinese very fluently, David does not speak fluently.|
|**8 (Example)**|Q: Zuótiān nǐmen wánr de zěnmeyàng?A: Zuótiān wǒ wánr de hěn kāixīn, tā wánr de bù kāixīn.|Q: How did you play/have fun yesterday?A: Yesterday I played very happily, he did not play happily.|
