Common part meaning: 

**1. Common Character: 长**

- **特长** (_tècháng_): strong point, speciality.
- **校长** (_xiàozhǎng_): school principal, president.
- **Meaning of the common part**: In these words, the common character represents a "speciality" or a "principal". _Outside knowledge: **长** is a polyphone. When pronounced "cháng", it means "long" or refers to a "specialty." When pronounced "zhǎng", it means "head," "chief," or "senior" (as in the head of a school)._

**2. Common Character: 大**

- **大夫** (_dàifu_): doctor.
- **大学** (_dàxué_): college, university.
- **Meaning of the common part**: In the source, this character is used in the terms for "doctor" and "college". _Outside knowledge: **大** generally means "big" or "large" (pronounced "dà"), which is why a university is literally a "big school". However, it takes on a special pronunciation ("dài") specifically in the traditional word for doctor._

**3. Common Character: 得**

- **觉得** (_juéde_): to think, to feel.
- **我得走了** (_wǒ děi zǒu le_): I have to go..
- **Meaning of the common part**: The source uses this character in an expression about "thinking/feeling" and to express "have to". _Outside knowledge: **得** is a highly versatile polyphone. When pronounced "děi," it acts as an auxiliary verb meaning "must" or "have to." When pronounced with a neutral tone ("de"), it is a structural particle attached to a verb, as in "juéde" (to feel)._

**4. Common Character: 行**

- **银行** (_yínháng_): bank.
- **举行** (_jǔxíng_): to hold.
- **Meaning of the common part**: The source demonstrates its use in a "bank" and the action "to hold" (an event). _Outside knowledge: **行** is a polyphone that means a "firm," "profession," or "line of business" when pronounced "háng" (hence silver/money firm = bank). When pronounced "xíng," it means "to go," "to do," or "to conduct/execute."_
