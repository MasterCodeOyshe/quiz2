Here is a comparison table that brings together the three types of sentences (Assertive, Interrogative/Question, and Negative) for each of the grammatical structures we discussed.

|Sentence Structure|Assertive (Statement)|Interrogative (Question)|Negative|
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
|**1. "把" (bǎ) Sentences**|**请把外套脱下来。__Qǐng bǎ wàitào tuō xiàlai._(Please take off your coat.)_|**你把手机放在哪儿了？__Nǐ bǎ shǒujī fàng zài nǎr le?_(Where did you put your cellphone?)_|**我没把手机放在桌子上。__Wǒ méi bǎ shǒujī fàng zài zhuōzi shang._(I didn't put the cellphone on the table.)_|
|**2. "会" (huì) Sentences**|**意大利队会赢。__Yìdàlì duì huì yíng._(The Italians will win.)_|**明天会下雨吗？__Míngtiān huì xià yǔ ma?_(Will it rain tomorrow?)_|**我下个星期不会去上海。__Wǒ xià ge xīngqī bú huì qù Shànghǎi._(I will not go to Shanghai next week.)_|
|**3. "了" (le) Sentences**|**我看了一场中文电影。__Wǒ kànle yì chǎng Zhōngwén diànyǐng._(I saw a Chinese movie.)_|**你借了几本杂志？__Nǐ jièle jǐ běn zázhì?_(How many magazines did you borrow?)_|**我没看中文电影。__Wǒ méi kàn Zhōngwén diànyǐng._(I didn't see a Chinese movie.)_|

Double-Verb Sentences**|**请校长发奖。__Qǐng xiàozhǎng fā jiǎng._(Let's welcome the school principal to present the awards.)_|**他想请女朋友做什么？__Tā xiǎng qǐng nǚpéngyou zuò shénme?_(What does he want to invite his girlfriend to do?)_|**老师没叫我回答这个问题。__Lǎoshī méi jiào wǒ huídá zhège wèntí._(The teacher didn't ask me to answer this question.)_ *|
|**5. Action Frequency**|**我打了三次电话。__Wǒ dǎle sān cì diànhuà._(I called three times.)_|**这个月你迟到了几次？__Zhège yuè nǐ chídàole jǐ cì?_(How many times were you late this month?)_|**我没打三次电话。__Wǒ méi dǎ sān cì diànhuà._(I didn't call three times.)_ *|
|**6. Action Duration**|**我学了两年汉语了。__Wǒ xuéle liǎng nián Hànyǔ le._(I've been learning Chinese for two years.)_|**你学了多长时间汉语了？__Nǐ xuéle duō cháng shíjiān Hànyǔ le?_(How long have you been learning Chinese?)_|**刘老师没睡八个小时。__Líu lǎoshī méi shuì bā ge xiǎoshí._(Teacher Liu didn't sleep for eight hours.)_ *|
|**7. "比" (bǐ) Comparisons**|**这个城市比那个城市暖和。__Zhège chéngshì bǐ nàge chéngshì nuǎnhuo._(This city is warmer than that city.)_|**哥哥高还是弟弟高？__Gēge gāo háishi dìdi gāo?_(Is the older brother taller or the younger brother?)_|**弟弟没有哥哥高。__Dìdi méiyǒu gēge gāo._(The younger brother is not as tall as the older brother.)_|
|**8. Question words + 也/都**|**什么都知道。__Shénme dōu zhīdào._(Knows everything.)_|_N/A (This structure itself uses question words to make a statement, so it doesn't have a direct question format)._|**爷爷什么也没买。__Yéye shénme yě méi mǎi._(Grandpa bought nothing.)_|
